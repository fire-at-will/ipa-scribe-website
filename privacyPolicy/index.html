Privacy Policy

This policy applies to all information collected or submitted on this website (the "Website") and the IPA Scribe app published by Will Taylor (the "App(s)").

Will Taylor ("us", "we", "our") takes personal privacy seriously. We limit the collection of personal information to cases where we truly need it or is required for the operation of our products. Personal information is never shared except to comply by the law, develop and improve our products, or protect our rights.

Your Personal Data

Nothing that you enter in IPA Scribe is viewable by us, period. All transcription processing is performed on your device and cannot be seen by anyone.

Information We Collect
Technical Basics

Our servers may store basic technical information, including but not limited to your IP address, in temporary memory or logs.

Debugging Data

We use Apple's opt-in crash reporting service to improve the quality of IPA Scribe. Your device type, OS version, and time of the crash will be viewable by us. No personally identifiable information is uploaded during this process.

Randomly Generated Identifiers

We use randomly generated identifiers to anonymously identify users. Your anonymous user identifier may be synced to your other devices via iCloud. Your anonymous ID is associated with data sent to RevenueCat and Mixpanel. While the ID can identify your device, we cannot trace or link it back to you without you providing the ID to us.

Email

If you email us for support or other feedback, the emails and email addresses will be retained. The email addresses will only be used to reply to the concerns or suggestions raised and will never be used for any marketing purpose.

User Generated Content

The transcriptions performed within the App are processed locally on your device and are not uploaded to a server or shared with anyone, including us, without your explicit permission.

Analytics and Purchase Data

In addition to the anonymous analytics data provided by Apple, we use Mixpanel, Amplitude, and Firebase to store anonymous analytics data. No personally identifiable or health information is uploaded, and the data cannot be traced back to you without your permission. You may view Mixpanel's privacy policy here, Firebase's privacy policy here, and Amplitude's privacy policy here.

We use Apple as a payment provider for paid subscriptions. Apple's Privacy Policy can be found here.

We use RevenueCat to manage trial and subscriptions purchases of IPA Scribe Pro. To manage these transactions, we send RevenueCat your anonymous user ID and the details of which item you are purchasing. Since Apple processes the transactions, no credit card, health, or personally identifiable information is ever sent to RevenueCat. You may view RevenueCat's privacy policy here.
